Sandbytes Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

This policy explains how Sandbytes (“we,” “us”) handles information across our websites and automations that post to social platforms. If you have questions, email contact@sandbytes.ai.

Information we collect

  • Content you provide: titles, captions, images, and links you upload to our tools.
  • Account and auth data: platform identifiers and OAuth tokens you explicitly connect so we can post on your behalf.
  • Usage and device logs: basic diagnostic logs such as timestamps, IP address, and user agent for security and abuse prevention.

How we use information

  • To create and publish posts you request.
  • To operate, secure, and improve our services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We share data with service providers only to run our services for example cloud hosting, file storage, and analytics. These providers process data under our instructions.

Data retention

We keep information only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, then delete or anonymize it. You can revoke connected accounts at any time in the respective platform. Revoking may stop posting.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards such as HTTPS in transit, access controls, and limited-scope tokens.

Your choices

  • Unlink social accounts directly on those platforms.
  • Request access or deletion by emailing contact@sandbytes.ai.

Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We will update this page if the policy changes. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
